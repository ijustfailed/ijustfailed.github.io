What is Failure?

Failure is not negative. It's often an opportunity! You're here at this webpage to learn more about it: follow through until the end to change how you approach failures.

Failure?

Failure is not always negative-- and the sooner you realize this the better prepared you will be for the future. But don't take it from us! Take it from these well-known individuals:

Thomas Edison

“I have not failed. I’ve just found 10,000 ways that won’t work.”

J.K. Rowling

“It is impossible to live without failing at something, unless you live so cautiously that you might as well not have lived at all- in which case, you fail by default”

Milton Hershey

Milton Hershey’s first two chocolate businesses went bankrupt, but his perseverance led him to be extremely successful with the third.

Winston Churchill

“Success is not final, failure is not fatal; it is the courage to continue that counts.”

Why Fail?

Failure forces you to: leave your comfort zone, experience new things, learn more in life, and grow. You'd rather be a heartbeat than a straight line, not only is there more variation, but there is excitement to look forward to and greater heights to reach.
